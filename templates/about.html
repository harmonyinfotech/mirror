About Magic Mirror

Magic Mirror is a simple yet powerful tool that makes text sharing between devices feel magical. No complex setup, no accounts needed - just open the page and watch your text sync instantly across all your devices.

How it Works

When you open Magic Mirror, it automatically detects your network and creates a temporary sharing room. Any device on the same network can access this room and see the shared content in real-time.

Privacy & Security

Your data is temporary and only accessible to devices on your network. Content expires after 24 hours and is never permanently stored. We don't track personal information or use cookies.

Open Source

Magic Mirror is open source and available on GitHub. Feel free to contribute or report issues!
