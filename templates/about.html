About mirror.is

mirror.is is a simple, fast, and secure way to share text between devices on the same network. No signup, no installation - just open and share.

How it Works

When you open mirror.is, it automatically detects your network and creates a temporary sharing room. Any device on the same network can access this room and see the shared content in real-time.

Privacy & Security

Your data is temporary and only accessible to devices on your network. Content expires after 24 hours and is never permanently stored. We don't track personal information or use cookies.

Open Source

mirror.is is open source and available on GitHub. Feel free to contribute or report issues!
